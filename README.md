## 📦 What is AndroHunter?

AndroHunter is a simple program designed to help you find hidden threats on your Android device. It scans your phone for suspicious apps and files that could cause harm. This tool works on Windows computers, making it easy to connect your Android device and check for issues.

You do not need any special skills or software knowledge to use AndroHunter. It guides you step-by-step, so you can run it safely and get results you can trust.

---

## 💻 System Requirements

Before you start, make sure your computer meets these requirements:

- Windows 10 or later (64-bit recommended)
- At least 4 GB of free disk space
- USB port for connecting your Android device
- Internet connection (for downloading AndroHunter)

Your Android device should be running Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or newer. You also need to enable USB Debugging on your phone. This lets the program scan your device safely.

---

## 🔧 How AndroHunter Works

1. Connect your Android phone to your Windows computer using a USB cable.
2. The program scans your device’s installed apps and files.
3. It looks for malware, spyware, or risky software hiding inside your phone.
4. You get a clear report showing what it found and what to do next.

The tool does not change or delete anything without your permission. It only helps you find problems.

## 🔌 Connect Your Android Device

Before running AndroHunter, prepare your phone:

1. Enable USB Debugging  
   - Open **Settings** on your phone.  
   - Go to **About Phone**.  
   - Tap **Build Number** seven times to unlock **Developer Options**.  
   - Return to **Settings**, open **Developer Options**.  
   - Find and turn on **USB Debugging**.

2. Use a USB cable to connect your phone to the Windows PC.

3. When prompted on your phone, allow the PC to access your device data.

---

## ▶️ Run AndroHunter

Once installation and connection are ready, launch AndroHunter:

- Open the program from your desktop or Start menu.
- The main window will show a button labeled **Start Scan**.
- Click **Start Scan** to begin.

The scan might take several minutes depending on the number of apps and files on your phone. You can watch the progress bar as the scan runs.

---

## 📝 Understanding Scan Results

When the scan finishes, AndroHunter shows a list of items found on your Android device.

- **Safe** means no issues were detected.
- **Suspicious** means the app or file may have risks.
- **Malicious** means the item is dangerous and could harm your phone.

For suspicious or malicious items, AndroHunter provides simple advice next to each item, such as:

- Uninstall the app from your device.
- Run a full antivirus scan on your phone.
- Change your passwords if you think your data was exposed.

You can export this report as a text file for your records.

## ⚙️ Troubleshooting Tips

- If AndroHunter cannot detect your device, check the USB connection and try a different USB port or cable.
- Make sure USB Debugging is turned on.
- Restart your computer and phone if the program freezes or crashes.
- Close other apps that might interfere with USB connection.
